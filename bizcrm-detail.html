  • System Overview

    Vtiger CRM is a cloud-based and open source CRM software developed by Vtiger that helps teams track leads, contacts, deals, and customer interactions. It offers tools for sales, marketing, and customer support in one platform.

    BizCRM is a Vtiger-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system designed for small to mid-sized businesses to manage customers, sales, and interactions in one place. It is fully customizable according to the customer's needs.
